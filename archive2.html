[About]
A production presented at the Brooklyn Academy of Music, Through Shifting Seasons is a multidisciplinary performance that reimagines Antonio Vivaldi's iconic symphony, The Four Seasons, composed in 1723. This innovative interpretation blends dance, video, and sound to create a transformative experience that bridges classical music with contemporary artistry.
