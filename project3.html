Hosted at The Cooper Union, Incorporations: The Body as Source Lecture Series is an intellectually provocative exploration of the intersection between humanity and its environment. This series examines whether the environment can transcend its traditional role as a passive container for human activity and instead be reconceived as an extension of the body itself—a living, interactive participant in our collective existence.
